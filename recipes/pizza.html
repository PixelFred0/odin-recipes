Pizza recipe

A tasty Pizza

It's a easy Recipe and everyone can make it at home

in just under one hour.

Ingredients:

  • 200ml Water
  • 500g Flour
  • 5g Jest
  • 1 tbs Salt

Steps:

  1. Put all Ingredients in a medium bowl and mix them

    until it is a even round dough ball.

  2. Let the dough rest in the fridge for at least one hour.
  3. Take the dough out of the fridge and roll it out in

    the size you want for your pizza.
